Intelli+save - Smart Plug

Smart plug is a high-performance plug that can be used to connect high-performance household appliances to the Smart Home network. It contains an energy measuring circuit for dynamic energy measurement, built-in overvoltage protection and a ceramic housing for electrical protection. It can be connected to multiple devices to be used effectively.

Features of Smart Plug

  • You can turn your 01 appliance as Intelligent appliance
  • It can safely operate for 6A and 16A electrical appliances.
  • Devices you can control with it are AC, Geyser, Kettle, Iron and Cofee Maker and many others
  • For connecting 6A / 16A devices. Rated voltage I / P: 230 V, 50 Hz.
  • It is used basically for for devices which have high power consumption.
  • It has built-in surge protection to protect expensive equipment from surges.
  • Performance monitoring functions to measure the power consumed by the device in real time.
  • You can communicate via WLAN, it works with / without the Internet.
  • It comes with manual switching functions.
  • Saves electricity and less monthly bill.
