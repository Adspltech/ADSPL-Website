Inelli+save – Switches

The Intellisave switch has been carefully designed to connect the existing switch board with ease and minimum wiring to provide different functions all around your home. Each switch contains a dimmer control along with normal switch controls. It comes with a connected sensor strip. You can use many features via one device. It also includes an energy metering circuit for displaying live energy consumption data. With the help of analysing the reactive and active power you can identify faulty appliances and take action to maintain or replace them to save energy. You can connect and monitor household appliances in real time by controlling LED/ RGB light, Fan, TV, Music system, DVD player, AC, Geyser, Refrigerator, Microwave etc all can be controlled by Smart Switch.

Intelli+save Switches

The Intelli+save Switches comes in 03 variations – 04 Nodes, 06 Nodes and 08 Nodes Every Controller contains a fan dimmer control along with normal switch controls. It houses a smart controller that allows you to control all common home appliances via the Intelli+save Intelligent Home app. It also contains a power measurement circuit for displaying live power consumption data of connected appliances in real time.

Features

  • In built surge protector to protect your appliances from both high and low voltages.
  • and wiring intact
  • Our Controllers can be installed inside the existing switches and are very cost effective and durable, No need of changing to new switches no extra cost
  • Manual switching – Can be still used
  • Comes with built-in dimmer for fans and dimmable LEDs. They can be used just like regular dimmers.
  • Retrofit module – existing wiring set-up need a little ramp up.
  • Our Intelli+save Voice can be used for better connectivity and control of appliances even if there is no Wifi
