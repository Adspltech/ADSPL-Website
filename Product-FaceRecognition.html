What Is A Facial Recognition Attendance System?

A facial recognition attendance system uses facial recognition technology to identify and verify a person using the person's facial features and automatically mark attendance.The software can be used for different groups of people such as employees, students, etc. The system records and stores the data in real-time.

What’s the hype all about?

A facial recognition attendance system is a contactless way to manage visitors and employees in an organization. Unlike other types of biometric systems, such as fingerprints that capture identity through touch, a facial recognition system is a touchless way to manage employees and visitors. In times of the COVID-19 pandemic, a touchless system is an effective preventive measure. It helps manage the inflow and outflow of people in buildings and premises in a safe and efficient manner.
