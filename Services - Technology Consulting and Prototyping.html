Technology Consulting & Prototyping

ADSPL do their inhouse work to identify the technical viability of the project and then define the project scope & plan to create a specification which will help in the building robust system architecture which will then support our solution engineers choose the best technologies and frame work for your team which is the best in the industry.

We check the product against the below vital parameters:

Needs

Must have, top priority

Wants

Nice to have

Desires

Would love to have someday

We then discuss provides open consultation on the risk associated and provide our recommendation to make the project viable. Our expert team helps customers choose prototyping tools and ready-made enclosure based on initial hardware requirements (type of solution, CPU, RAM, power consumption, connectivity) and create a detailed product roadmap. We also conduct a preliminary technical, market and Bill of Materials (BOM) analysis and provide you with ballpark hardware design and production estimates to increase your chances of getting funded.

We believe that prototyping will help you see what you desire and can help you shape and finalize your decision.
